Sports

written by Jack Dean


Looking back at the past few years at university, some of the highlight moments have been whilst playing sports. Whether that’s netball, swimming, volleyball or trying out a new sport, the adrenaline and spirit that is released during these games are underappreciated. Sport is a fantastic way to get out of the library, to meet new people and get some fresh air. It is especially important during exam time, when you need a break from the revision. Many students may say that they don’t have the time for sports during exam, but I feel that it key part of keeping a healthy mind. Whether that’s a walk to the Clifton Suspension Bridge, or a quick jog around the block, it is important to stay healthy and stay fit. Not only can it help maintain your mood, but it can also help increase your focus. By breaking up the day with some sport, it can help you sleep better meaning you feel more revitalised for the following day.
