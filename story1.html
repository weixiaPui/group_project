Final Year Thoughts

written by Annie F


I’m in third year and I’m staying for another year but most of my friends are not and oh hell! I’m going to miss them. We waste all of our time watching stupid cake videos but that’s ok because we all love each other. I feel like we are all big kids at the moment but I can’t wait to see them flourish into beautiful adults and see how they will conquer all the challenges ahead!

University has been an absolute rollercoaster from fresh-faced freshers when we were always up for a good time. To classy second year in Clifton where everyday felt like we were on the set of Notting Hill and my one of my best male friends got really fat because Co-op was really close by and he’s addicted to cheese twists and pizza swirlies. Now to year three, we are finally becoming adults. Applying for internships and jobs have got us all clinching our teeth but we still have time for a quick drink at the pub and movie nights at the Watershed.
